# CarSales-Dashboard


## Project Description

The **Car Sales Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence solution designed to analyze and visualize **Year-to-Date (YTD) car sales performance**, pricing trends, customer segmentation, and company-wise sales contribution.

This dashboard helps stakeholders quickly understand:

* Overall revenue performance
* Average vehicle pricing trends
* Total cars sold
* Sales distribution by **body style, color, gender, and company**
* Weekly sales movement across the year

It is built to support **data-driven decision making** for automobile sales management.

---

## Key Insights Displayed

### 1. Sales Performance KPIs

* **YTD Total Sales**
* **Month-to-Date (MTD) Sales**
* **YTD Average Price**
* **Total Cars Sold**
* Percentage growth comparison with previous period

These KPIs provide a **quick executive summary** of current performance.

---

### 2. Trend Analysis

* **Weekly YTD Sales Trend Line Chart**

  * Identifies peak demand weeks
  * Highlights seasonal fluctuations
  * Helps forecast upcoming sales patterns

---

### 3. Sales Distribution Analysis

* **Sales by Body Style (Donut Chart)**

  * SUV, Hatchback, Sedan, Passenger, Hardtop
  * Shows customer vehicle preference

* **Sales by Color (Donut Chart)**

  * Pale White, Black, Red
  * Helps understand styling trends affecting demand

---

### 4. Customer Segmentation

* **Sales by Gender (Bar Chart)**

  * Compares purchasing behavior between **Male** and **Female** customers
  * Useful for targeted marketing strategies

---

### 5. Company-Wise Sales Performance

Interactive table displaying:

* YTD Average Price
* Cars Sold
* Total Revenue
* % Contribution to Total Sales

This enables **brand-level performance comparison** and competitive analysis.
